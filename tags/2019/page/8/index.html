Speaker - Namrata Bandekar

Twitter - https://twitter.com/NamrataCodes

Bio - Namrata is a Principal Software Engineer at Zynga building addictive games. She is also a member of the Ray Wenderlich Tutorial Team. Abstract - ARKit has shown how AR can be integrated into apps to make them more engaging and fun. If you are thinking about using ARKit in your app, this talk is for you.
